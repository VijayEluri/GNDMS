Use Pandoc to convert the md files:

Either:

pandoc --from=markdown --to=mediawiki installation-guide/index.md \
architecture-primer/index.md developer-guide/index.md \
glossary/index.md imprint/index.md index.md moni-guide/index.md \
license/index.md -o all_raw.wiki

Or the detour over html:

pandoc --from=markdown --to=htmli installation-guide/index.md \
architecture-primer/index.md developer-guide/index.md \
glossary/index.md imprint/index.md index.md moni-guide/index.md \
license/index.md -o all.html

pandoc --from=html --to=mediawiki all.html -o all_raw.wiki

Check wich of the two is closer to an error-free mediawiki file.

Use sed script do beautify the result: 

sed -file conv.sed all_raw.wiki > all.wiki

Links must be fixed manually.
